## WORKSHEET 5: COST PER USE
**Name:__________________ Date:______________**

**Understanding Cost Per Use: Divide total cost by number of times used**

### Examples:

**Gaming Headset:**
- Cost: $80
- Use it every day for 2 years (730 days)
- $80 √∑ 730 = $0.11 per day
- **Worth it? YES** (You use it a lot!)

**Fancy Shoes:**
- Cost: $120
- Wear them 2 times, then sit in closet
- $120 √∑ 2 = $60 per wear
- **Worth it? NO** (Too expensive for actual use)
