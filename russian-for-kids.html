Why Russian?

Why Parents Are Choosing
Russian for Their Kids

With over 300 million speakers and growing global importance, Russian is one of the most strategic language investments you can make for your child's future.

🌍

One of the world's major languages

Russian is spoken across 15+ countries and is one of the six official UN languages. A child who learns Russian opens doors across Eastern Europe, Central Asia, and beyond.

🧠

Children learn languages fastest

The window for natural language acquisition is wide open in childhood. Starting Russian young means better pronunciation, faster fluency, and effortless retention later in life.

🔤

A new alphabet is a new superpower

Learning Cyrillic gives children cognitive flexibility and confidence that transfers to every other area of learning. It's not just a language — it's a mental workout.

🏆

Stand out in any classroom

Very few children in Western schools learn Russian. Your child will have a uniquely rare and impressive skill that sets them apart at every level of education.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age is Russian for Kids designed for?
The course is designed for children from approximately age 5–6 upward. Younger children will need parental involvement; children aged 8+ can often work through many sections more independently.
Does the course include the Cyrillic alphabet?
Yes — learning the Cyrillic alphabet is one of the first things covered. The course takes children from zero knowledge, teaching each letter clearly before moving into vocabulary and conversation.
Does the parent/teacher need to speak Russian?
No prior Russian knowledge is needed. The course is designed to guide both the child and the adult through the material together. Everything is explained clearly in English.
How does this work as a homeschool curriculum?
The individual license is perfect for homeschoolers. The course is self-contained, structured, and designed to work at home at your own pace. Many homeschool families use it as their complete Russian language program.
What's included in the school license?
The school license covers unlimited use across the whole school — all teachers, all classes, all students. It's a one-time investment to launch a Russian language program school-wide.
