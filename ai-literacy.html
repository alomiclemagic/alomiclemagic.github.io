Frequently Asked Questions

What age is this course for?
The course is designed for children ages 8–14+. The language is accessible for younger kids in that range with parental support, and engaging enough for older students and even adults who are new to AI concepts.
Does my child need to have used AI before?
Absolutely not. The course starts from zero — assuming no prior knowledge. It's designed to be a first introduction that builds confidence gradually across 25 lessons.
Is this just about Claude, or about AI in general?
While Claude is the primary AI used in examples, the principles taught apply to all AI systems — ChatGPT, Gemini, and any AI your child will encounter in the future. The goal is understanding AI, not just one tool.

The Individual license is for home use with your own children. The Teacher license is for one teacher's classroom. The School license covers unlimited teachers and students across a whole school or district.
