Elena & Lexi's
Adventure Day

A mother-daughter nature escape

📍 Cheile Nerei–Beușnița National Park, Romania
🗺️

The Day Plan

7:00
AM
🚗 Departure

Leave Timișoara

Pack the car the night before. Grab coffees & car snacks, put on a road trip playlist and hit the road. The Banat countryside is beautiful in the morning light.

9:15
AM
🚗 Arrival

Enter the National Park

Park at Podu Bei. Pay the small entrance fee in cash (~10–20 RON per car). Lace up boots, apply sunscreen, and breathe that mountain air. 🌲

9:30
AM
🌊 First Stop

La Văioaga Waterfall

Your warm-up wonder — a gorgeous waterfall just a short walk from the parking. Two paths lead down to it; let Lexi choose! The forest mist and turquoise water will wake you both right up.

10:45
AM
⭐ Highlight · 4.9 Stars

Ochiul Beiului — The Blue Eye Lake

A 30-minute easy forest trail leads to this electric-blue karst lake. Round, impossibly vivid, fed by underground springs. Lexi will gasp. This is your picnic spot — spread your blanket, unpack your basket, and soak in the magic. Stay as long as you like. 💙

1:00
PM
🌊 Second Stop

Beușnița Waterfalls

A short walk from the lake takes you to a series of cascading moss-covered falls. On sunny days the mist creates tiny rainbows. Adventurous but completely doable for Lexi — some stepping stones make it feel like a real quest!

2:30
PM
🚗 Heading Home

Back via Sasca Montană

Stop in the village for a cold drink and let Lexi pick a souvenir if she spots one. Then the scenic 2.5-hour drive home to Timișoara — tired, sunlit, and full of stories. 🐾

🚗

Drive Route

Timișoara → Reșița → Oravița → Sasca Montană → Podu Bei 🏞️
Total Distance
~150 km
Drive Time
~2.5 hrs
Last Road
Dirt / 4x4

⚠️ Important: The last few km into the park from Sasca Montană are on an unpaved road. It's manageable in a regular car if driven slowly — but if you have an SUV, even better. Download offline Google Maps for this route before leaving. Fill up on fuel in Reșița or Oravița — no petrol stations near the park. Bring cash for the entrance fee, water top-up, and Terasa Bei (the only small eatery on the trail).

Picnic Menu

🥪 Mains

  • Sandwiches cu pui și roșii (Lexi's fave!)
  • Baguette with cream cheese & cucumber
  • Hard-boiled eggs with salt
  • Cheese & salami roll-ups

🍓 Snacks & Sides

  • Cherry tomatoes & baby carrots
  • Strawberries or grapes (easy to eat)
  • Crackers with hummus
  • Trail mix with raisins & nuts

🍫 Lexi's Treats

  • Chocolate squares (not bars — they melt!)
  • Wafer biscuits
  • Dried mango or apricots
  • Corn puffs or pretzels

💧 Drinks

  • Water — at least 1.5L per person
  • Thermos of tea or coffee (for you!)
  • Juice box for Lexi
  • Electrolyte drink for the hike
💡 Pro tip: Pack everything in reusable containers the night before and keep in the fridge. In the car, use a cooler bag with an ice pack — it'll still be fresh by lake time. Don't forget a picnic blanket, napkins, and a small trash bag to leave no trace! 🌿
