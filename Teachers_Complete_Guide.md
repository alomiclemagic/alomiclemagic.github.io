## üí° TEACHING TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### Make It Relevant
- Use current events and trends
- Reference students' favorite games, shows, celebrities
- Connect to their actual daily experiences
- Show real consequences and benefits

### Keep It Interactive
- Use role-plays and scenarios extensively
- Have discussions and debates
- Do partner/group activities
- Minimize lecturing
- Make it FUN

### Differentiate Your Instruction
- **For younger students (8-10):**
  - Simpler language and concepts
  - More concrete examples
  - Shorter activities
  - More visuals
  - Less writing

- **For older students (13-16):**
  - Complex scenarios and applications
  - Research components
  - Written reflections
  - Leadership opportunities
  - Real-world applications

### Manage Classroom Dynamics
1. Set clear expectations
2. Be consistent
3. Celebrate effort
4. Address issues promptly
5. Build a safe, judgment-free zone
6. Model the behavior you teach

## üìä ASSESSMENT STRATEGY

### Formative Assessment (Throughout)
- **Daily:** Observe behavior and participation
- **Weekly:** Collect worksheet responses
- **Ongoing:** Note engagement in activities
- Purpose: Monitor understanding and adjust teaching

### Summative Assessment (Final)
- **Unit Quizzes:** Check understanding of each topic
- **Final Test:** Comprehensive assessment of all material
- **Project:** Personal plan or pledge
- **Presentation:** Optional student presentations
- Purpose: Grade and document learning

### Grading Recommendations

- Worksheets: 20%
- Quizzes: 30%
- Participation/Behavior: 25%
- Final Project/Test: 25%
