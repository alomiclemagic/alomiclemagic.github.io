Frequently Asked Questions

What age is this guide designed for?
The guide works across a wide age range — from ages 5–6 (with parental guidance) all the way through early teens. Different modules suit different ages, and the guide is designed to grow with the child.
Do I need a financial background to teach this?
Not at all. The guide is written in clear, accessible language that any parent or teacher can use — regardless of their own financial knowledge. Many parents find they learn alongside their children.
Is this curriculum-aligned?
The guide covers the core financial literacy concepts recommended by most educational frameworks worldwide, making it a natural complement to any existing curriculum. It's been designed to slot in flexibly rather than replace anything.
