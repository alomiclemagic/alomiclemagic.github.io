Important rules when using AI:

🔐 Ethical AI Use in Education

Best practices:
✅ Always review AI-generated content for accuracy
✅ Use AI to supplement, not replace, your expertise
✅ Teach students about AI - don't hide that you use it
✅ Never input student personal information into AI tools
✅ Maintain your authentic teaching voice and style

💡 Teacher Time-Saver: Use AI for the structure and foundation, then add YOUR personality, examples, and expertise. AI handles the tedious stuff - YOU bring the magic! ✨
