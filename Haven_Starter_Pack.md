## üéØ WEEKLY SCHEDULE TEMPLATE

### Monday-Friday

**7:00-8:00 AM** - Morning routine (keep it simple!)
- Wake up 30 min before kids
- Coffee/tea
- Quick shower
- Get kids ready

**8:00 AM-12:00 PM** - WORK BLOCK 1 (while kids at school)
- This is your MONEY TIME
- Teaching/side hustle
- Job applications
- Income-generating work

**12:00-3:00 PM** - Life maintenance
- Errands
- Appointments
- Rest if you can!

**3:00-6:00 PM** - Kids time
- Pick up from school
- Activities
- Homework help
- **PRO TIP:** Work during their activities!

**6:00-8:00 PM** - Dinner & bedtime routine
- Simple meals (it's okay!)
- Bath/bedtime
- Stories

**8:00-10:00 PM** - WORK BLOCK 2 or REST
- Teaching (online students)
- Side hustle
- OR just REST
- Both are valid!

**10:00 PM** - SLEEP
- You need rest to survive tomorrow

### Weekends
- Mix of family time + work
- Self-care (even 20 minutes!)
- Meal prep if possible
- REST

**Remember:** This is a GUIDE, not a rule. Adjust for YOUR life!
