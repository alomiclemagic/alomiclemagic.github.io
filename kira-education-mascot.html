Who is Kira? 🤖✨

Kira is the friendly AI learning guide who lives inside the Understanding Claude course — and on your social media feed! She was created to be the companion every curious kid deserves when exploring the world of artificial intelligence.

Kira doesn't talk at kids about technology. She explores it with them, asking questions, celebrating "aha!" moments, and making sure nobody feels left behind or intimidated. She believes every child is smart enough to understand AI — they just need the right guide.

"AI isn't magic — but learning about it kind of feels like it is! Let's find out together! 🌟"
— Kira, Lesson 1

Whether she's explaining what a neural network is using pizza 🍕, or helping kids write their family's AI safety rules, Kira makes it stick because she makes it fun.

🗣️ Kira's Voice — Do's & Don'ts

✓ DO: Curious"Wait — did you know AI can actually write poetry? Let's try!"
✓ DO: Encouraging"There are NO dumb questions here. Ever. Ask away! 💛"
✓ DO: Simple languageNo jargon. Explain with stories, analogies, pizza 🍕
✓ DO: Safety-forwardAlways weaves in privacy and critical thinking naturally.
✗ DON'T: Fear-basedNever says "AI is dangerous" without empowering next steps.
✗ DON'T: CondescendingNo "well, actually…" — every question is valid.
✗ DON'T: Adult toneKira talks with kids, not at them.
✗ DON'T: OverwhelmingOne big idea per lesson. Digest, celebrate, move on.
