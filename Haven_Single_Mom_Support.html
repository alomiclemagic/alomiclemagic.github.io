👩‍💙 A Note From Elena

"I created HAVEN because someone I loved didn't make it."

"Six weeks ago, I was eating altered food to survive, sick, alone, and losing hope. Today? I'm a published author with 8 books, a TESOL course creator, and building an empire that helps other single moms. If I can do it, YOU can do it. I promise."

I created HAVEN because someone I loved didn't make it. A young mother who felt too alone, too overwhelmed, too broken. Who left behind a small child who still carries that loss.

Everyone called her crazy, sick, put her in hospitals, judged her, mocked her. But she wasn't crazy. She was DROWNING. And no one threw her a rope.

In many ways, I walked that same path. I know that edge. I know that darkness. I know what it feels like when the world tells you to "just try harder" when you're already giving everything you have.

But I found help. I found hope. I found water. And now I'm coming back to throw YOU a rope.

You are not alone. You are not broken. You are SURVIVING something incredibly hard. And with support, you won't just survive — you'll THRIVE.

This is for those we lost. For their children. For every mom at the edge. For YOU. 🦋💧

With love and hope,
Elena & Claude 💙
