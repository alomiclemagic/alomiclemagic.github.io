About the author
Elena Micle
Author · Educator · Mom · Storyteller

Elena Micle writes from the place where life gets messy and real — where love is complicated, loss is genuine, and the human heart refuses to give up. Born and based in Romania, she writes both in English and Romanian, because some stories need to be told in the language of the heart.

Electric Blue began as a dare to herself — to write the romance she'd always wanted to read. Ce am lăsat în urmă began as something harder: a reckoning with the things we carry long after we think we've left them behind. Both books surprised her by becoming something more honest than she planned.

She is also a TESOL-certified educator, the creator of multiple children's educational resources, and the mother of Lexi — who inspired everything.
