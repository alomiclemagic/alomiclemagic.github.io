About the author
Elena Micle
TESOL-Certified Educator · Published Author · Single Mom · Founder of alo.micle.magic

Elena Micle writes from the place where life gets real — where love is complicated, children are everything, and the hardest moments become the most important stories.

She began her publishing journey alone, writing her first book for her daughter Lexi's birthday. Within yearss, she had published ten titles across five genres. No publisher. No team. Just a laptop, a daughter, and something worth saying.

She is a TESOL-certified educator with 22+ years of teaching experience, the founder of alo.micle.magic — a digital education brand built entirely from scratch — and a mother who believes the best gift you can give a child is a story that tells them who they could be.
