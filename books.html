About the author
Elena Micle
TESOL-Certified Educator · Published Author · Single Mom · Founder of alo.micle.magic

Elena Micle writes from the place where life gets real — where love is complicated, children are everything, and the hardest moments become the most important stories.

She began writing professionally in January 2026, publishing her first book Stories for My Daughter for her daughter Lexi's 10th birthday. Within months she had published seven titles across four genres — romance, children's AI literacy, parenting journals, and Romanian fiction.

She is a TESOL-certified educator with 22+ years of teaching experience, the founder of alo.micle.magic, and a mother who believes that the best thing you can give a child is a story that tells them who they could be.
